How To Play

Solve the clues, then place the answers into the outer circle so the last letter of one answer is the first letter of the next.

Start after the black square and travel clockwise. The thick-border boxes are shared chain letters. The black square marks the break in this open-chain version.

Each clue shows its extraction guide, such as 7(3): a 7-letter answer, taking the 3rd letter for the centre strip.

Variations

This demo shows the easy open-chain version. Other versions can use a closed circle, fewer centre-strip guides, mixed clue themes, or no guides at all.
